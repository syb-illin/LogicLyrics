# Code of Conduct

Logic Lyrics is a project for musicians and developers with different backgrounds, languages and levels of technical experience. Everyone participating in the repository, Discussions, issues and pull requests is expected to help keep the space constructive.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific and patient.
- Critique ideas and code, never people.
- Welcome questions and accessibility feedback without condescension.
- Respect privacy and creative ownership. Never repost another person's lyrics, audio, project files or identifying information.
- Accept moderation decisions and corrections in good faith.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, deliberate intimidation, personal attacks, doxxing, spam and publishing private creative material without permission are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior threatens the safety or usefulness of the community. Reports should be sent privately through the maintainer contact shown on the GitHub profile. Do not open a public issue containing sensitive evidence.

Reports will be reviewed as promptly and impartially as possible. Details will be shared only with people who need them to resolve the situation.
