One focused workflow

Less file archaeology. More music.

01

Recover

Open a .logicx project and extract Project Notes, BPM, key and section markers.

02

Shape

Edit lyrics, repair structure, copy individual sections and keep a local song history.

03

Prompt

Generate separate Styles, Styles to Exclude and Lyrics blocks for ChatGPT, Gemini and Suno.

04

Finish

Inspect and write MP3/WAV metadata, preview artwork and convert WAV exports with LAME.

Voice identity first

Prompts that protect the singer.

Logic Lyrics makes the vocal constraints explicit: preserve natural grain, chest voice and formants; avoid falsetto, screaming, singer replacement, unrealistic high notes and vocal acrobatics. Backing vocals remain optional, including female backing vocals.

Private by construction

Your songs stay on your Mac.

No app telemetry. Logic Lyrics never sends lyrics, prompts, audio, project names or paths.

No API key required. Copy the generated prompt into ChatGPT or Gemini yourself.

Originals preserved. Metadata and experimental Logic Notes writing always target a copy.

Ready when you are

Bring your project notes back into the song.
